[Who Built Bryn Mawr?](https://wbbm.digitalprojects.brynmawr.edu/) is an ongoing project that supports research and curatorial opportunities for students interested in changing the way the College understands its history. Over its 135+ years, the College has been sustained by countless individuals — not just the presidents, trustees, and donors memorialized on campus. The project aims to tell the stories of the people whose contributions have been forgotten, or too long overlooked.

In Summer 2023 I joined the WBBM team as the web developer for the website for the 2023-2026 iteration of the project. In addition to designing and building the website for the latest iteration of the project, I have been working on converting the websites for past iterations of the project from dynamic Wordpress sites to static Jekyll sites. This will make the sites more secure, easier to maintain and preserve, and more energy efficient.
