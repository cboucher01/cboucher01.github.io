title: The Encyclopedia of the Dog
---

I am the front-end web developer for [The Encyclopedia of the Dog](https://encyclopediaofthedog.com), an annotated digital edition of Sasha Sokolov's *Between Dog and Wolf*. 

I was initially hired to add the contents of the book (both the original Russian text and its English translation) to the annotation site and to clean and proofread the text on the site.
