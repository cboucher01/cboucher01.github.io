I am a part of the Educational and Scholarly Technology (EAST) Team at [[Bryn Mawr College]] as an EAST Assistant. EAST is a department within Library and Information Technology Services (LITS).

EAST helps students, faculty and staff integrate technology into teaching, learning, and research, and we provide a wide variety of services to the college community. The team's areas of focus include:
- [Blended Learning](https://www.brynmawr.edu/inside/offices-services/blended-learning)
- [Digital Competencies](https://www.brynmawr.edu/inside/offices-services/digital-competencies)
- [Digital Scholarship](https://digitalscholarship.blogs.brynmawr.edu/)
- Educational Technology
- Makerspace and Critical Making
- Assistive Technology and Accessibility
